Agora Atrium — Client Portal Platform

Agora Data Driven — agency operations & client dashboards hub

A multi-tenant platform that gives the agency one home base — "the Atrium" — to manage every client. Creating a client auto-generates a login and a blank, co-branded workspace; each client then gets live Looker Studio dashboards alongside lead generation, organic content, a content calendar, client communications, market intelligence and website-health views.
